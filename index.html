Privacy Policy

Depop Relister Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: May 12, 2025

Depop Relister is a Chrome extension that lets you bulk-relist your Depop listings from a side panel injected into depop.com. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Access

Depop Authentication Token

The extension reads your access_token cookie from depop.com solely to authenticate API requests made on your behalf (fetching your listings, re-uploading photos, creating new listings, and deleting old ones). This token is used in-memory for the duration of your session and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server we operate beyond the Depop API itself.

Depop Listing Data

The extension reads your Depop profile (username, user ID, avatar) and your listings (titles, descriptions, photos, pricing, sizes, and other listing attributes) in order to perform the relist operation. This data is fetched directly from Depop's API and is not stored on our servers.

Account Information (Email Address)

If you create an account to access premium features, we collect your email address via Supabase (our authentication and database provider). Your email is used to identify your account, manage your subscription plan, and send transactional messages (e.g., OTP codes for sign-in). We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

Listing Descriptions and Photos (AI Feature)

If you use the optional AI description optimization feature, the text of your listing description and (optionally) a product photo URL are sent to a Cloudflare Worker proxy we operate, which forwards the request to Google's Gemini API to generate a rewritten description. No data from this request is stored on our servers after the response is returned to you.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history or activity outside of depop.com.
  • We do not track which listings you view or interact with beyond what is needed to perform the relist action.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.
  • We do not use any analytics SDKs or tracking pixels.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Depop API — to fetch and manage your listings on your behalf.
  • Supabase — for account authentication and subscription plan storage. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Gemini API (via our Cloudflare Worker proxy) — used only when you invoke the AI description feature. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Amazon S3 — Depop uses S3 to store product photos. The extension fetches existing photos from S3 and uploads re-encoded copies as part of the relist flow.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account email address and subscription plan status in Supabase for as long as you maintain an account. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

5. Security

All communication between the extension and Depop's API, Supabase, and our Cloudflare Worker proxy uses HTTPS. Your Depop access token is never written to disk or transmitted outside of the request for which it is needed.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
